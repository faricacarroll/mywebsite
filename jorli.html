Jorli

Anti-Thef Alarm Wireframes

Jorli’s Anti-Theft Alarm acts as a motion alarm and protects your phone by sounding an alarm when someone moves your device or unplugs the charger from the outlet. When the alarm is triggered, a photo is taken and sent to your email address. I was asked to develop the initial wireframes for a website application that would collect all the data from the application. This would include phones, photos, and geolocation.
