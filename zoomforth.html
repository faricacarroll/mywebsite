Zoomforth

Page Analytics Wireframe

Visual Design/UX/UI

Zoomforth is an online platform which allows users to quickly assemble any content: media, text, videos, files, etc., into experiences called “Pages”, customize and share with a target audience, and track engagement in real time.

I created the final mock-up for their analytics page. I conceptualized various design methods to best visualize the page analytics. I performed a competitive analysis of other successful applications during the design brainstorming. I repeatedly engaged with the engineering team for feedback and made continuous reiterations.
