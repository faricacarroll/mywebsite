Inzopa

Website Wireframes

Inzopa is a private financial data network that stores financial data for sharing with financial services. The network is primarily used by homebuyers, homeowners, and banks for real estate and mortgage transactions.

The original website suffered in contrast of elements and cluttering of content on the homepage. There was not enough of a main focal point for the user to be directed towards. I worked on the initial wireframes under the main designer at Hackers / Founders to essentially redesign their website. This project taught me how it’s important to emphasize what you want the user to see, and that you can’t display too much information on one page or it will overwhelm the user. Also, white space is a great way to separate information.
