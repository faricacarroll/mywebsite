Jupyter

Brand Guide Book

Project Jupyter lacked in a consistent design theme across the board. There were a dozen different variations of the logo used in inconsistent ways. Two other designers and I decided to redefine our brand guidelines to develop a cohesive theme for all future use. We began with creating a new version of the logo and then created this brand guideline book to standardize all design specifications.
